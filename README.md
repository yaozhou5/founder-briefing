# Founder Briefing

A Claude skill that teaches you business through the news — one story at a time.

Say "briefing" every morning. Claude searches today's business news, picks 3 stories, and teaches you a business concept through each one. No onboarding. No setup. Just say the word.

## What it does

- Finds today's top business stories
- Breaks each one into: what happened → why it matters → business concept to remember
- Connects stories back to what you're building
- Suggests a content angle you can post about today
- Auto-saves every briefing to a tracker you can review later

## After one week you'll know

- Why Nike's "great" earnings were actually bad (quality of earnings)
- Why Comcast is worth more as 3 companies than 1 (conglomerate discount)
- Why SpaceX paid $60B for Cursor instead of building their own (acqui-hire at scale)
- Why Vuori's founder only raised money when he didn't need it (raise from strength)

## Install

1. Copy the contents of [`SKILL.md`](./SKILL.md)
2. Go to [Claude](https://claude.ai) → tap your name → **Customize** → **Skills**
3. Tap **+** → **Create Skill** → paste it in
4. Start a new chat and say **"briefing"**

That's it. No API keys. No config. Just paste and go.

## Trigger phrases

- `briefing`
- `daily briefing`
- `teach me business`
- `what should I know today`
- `brief me`
- `show my briefing log` (to review past sessions)

## Who it's for

Founders, designers, and engineers who are building products but never studied business. The kind of people who can ship the thing but want to think about it more strategically.

## Philosophy

Most business news tells you *what happened*. This skill teaches you *why it matters* and *how to think about it*. The goal is pattern recognition — over time you start seeing strategy, pricing, and market dynamics in everything you read.

> "Be a source, not a relay." — Grant Sanderson
